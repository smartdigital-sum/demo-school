About Sunrise Public School

Founded in 2005, Sunrise Public School has established itself as a leading institution committed to holistic education and character development. Our school provides a nurturing environment where every student is encouraged to excel academically, socially, and emotionally.

Mission

To foster an educational environment that cultivates critical thinking, creativity, and compassion in every student.

Vision

To nurture responsible global citizens who are equipped to lead and innovate in an ever-changing world.

Key Achievements

  • 95% Pass Rate in Board Exams
  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified
  • State-level Sports Champions
  • CBSE Affiliated School

Principal's Message

"At Sunrise Public School, we believe that education extends far beyond textbooks. Academic Programs

Pre-KG to KG

Foundation years focusing on play-based learning, cognitive development, and social skills.

  • English & Hindi
  • Mathematics Basics
  • Art & Craft
  • Physical Education

Classes I to V (Primary)

Focus on fundamental concepts with a balanced curriculum promoting creativity and analytical thinking.

  • English, Hindi, Math
  • Science & Social Studies
  • Computer Education
  • Co-curricular Activities

Transitional phase with emphasis on exploration and skill development across various subjects.

  • Advanced Science Labs
  • Mathematics & Languages

  • Humanities Stream
  • Competitive Exam Prep

Admissions

Join Sunrise Public School - Where Excellence Meets Opportunity

Eligibility Criteria

  • Pre-KG: Age 2.5 - 3.5 years
  • KG: Age 3.5 - 4.5 years
  • Class I: Age 5+ years | Must have attended KG
  • Other Classes: Merit-based admission | Entrance test required

Admission Process

  1. Fill online admission form on our portal
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Attend school visit and interaction
  4. Entrance test (if applicable)
  5. Merit declaration and counseling
  6. Final registration and fee submission

Admission Schedule 2024-25

  • Entrance Tests: February-March 2024
  • Merit Declaration: March 31, 2024
  • Admission Confirmation: April-May 2024
  • Session Starts: June 2024
